Meet Nitro Ninja

Meet Nitro Ninja, the newest recruit to help SapientNitro get the best budget estimates for their clients. Only Nitro Ninja has the skills to assassinate the evil forces of code bugs, comic sans, and more, in the fastest time possible.

 

As a masked ninja with a gender ambiguous name, their characteristics are unknown which allows the gamer to relate to Nitro Ninja as male, female, agender, bigender, etc. We wanted to make Nitro Run as inclusive as possible within our limited timeframe, which is why we made Nitro Ninja’s skin tone a desaturated hue from Nitro Ninja’s orange logo. We also included as many hair colors, hairstyles, and skin tones as possible to represent the company’s diverse and unique employees.
