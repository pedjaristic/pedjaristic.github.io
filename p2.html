Atomic | Zero

Branding / UX / Front-End — Designer

Project length: 3 months

Delivered: Branding | Visual Mockup Flows | Prototype | iOS UI Components

Atomic is a foundry fund focused on creating companies which solve world problems. https://atomic.vc

Output

Zero is an iOS app made to help professionals compose emails in a concise, and accurate manner. I worked with engineers; creating prototypes, and production-ready assets.

Problem

Zero began as a complaint from Jack Abraham (Atomic CEO) about the unresponsiveness of invested startup founders.

Zero was designed with a two stage approach in mind. I came up with initial concepts and after a quick email, I was working with Atomic to create Zero.

01 — Record quick audio response | Attaches to email | Assistant hears recording and types response | recipient recieves full response
02 — Record quick audio response | Attaches to email | A.I. composes response | Recipient recieves full response
Interaction Design

Interaction and animation played a key role in describing the concept to users.

Execution

After a zero-to-hero crash course on Swift and iOS, I was working with engineers to develop the Zero UI.

I created the Zero branding and motion design guidelines for the product.

Zero is now used internally to wrangle responses and garner an active community among Atomic founders.

Branding

I conducted a logo redesign, and created guidelines around the Atomic company brand.
