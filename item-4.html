The Vision

PVC's vision is to build the next generation of student leaders to tackle the world's challanges, create innovative solutions, and make a positive impact using technology.

How do we do it?

From educational tech talks, to innovation treks at the biggest compamies; PVC's main goal is to inspire big thinking! The Hacker House project, for example, is a product incubator run in Seattle where Waterloo students can venture to create the next big product.

My Role

From designing the logos and graphics for various projects and events to ideating and innovating new ones. Product Vision is structured similarily to a startup. It's hard to define a role as each director has complete ownership of their ideas. I personally had a hand in each one of the clubs initiatives from branding to logistics.

OnePM

I am personally owning the branding, design, and ideation of Waterloo's first product-minded hackathon along with the logistics and sponsorship teams. OnePM is a hackathon where students will be able to create a product idea and prototype that they can then take beyond the hackathon to further with support from PVC. OnePM focuses on Product Management and multi-disciplanary teamwork.

Pictured below are my iterations on the OnePM logo/brand and the in-development application website.

OnePM is partnered with the creators of Hack The North and is slated for Winter 2016. Applications will be open soon.
