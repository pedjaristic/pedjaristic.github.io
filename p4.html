HYLO

Branding / UX / Visual / Lead Development

Project length: 24 hours

Delivered: Branding | Visual Mockups | Functional Android App | Business Case

HYLO is a one to two person timing based mobile game designed to invoke feelings of zen and stress in tandem. Play a web version here.

Output

Collaborated with visual designers on art direction and style for multiple versions of HYLO.I designed the overall flow, interface, and mechanics of the mobile game.

Analysis

Researched and conceptualized the game concept, visual style, and game design patterns.

Scraped app store comments to gather user feedback on similar titles.

EXTENSIBLE DESIGN

HYLO was designed to have simple one touch mechanics which could easily be skinned with different styles.

As part of the business stratefy for Hylo, multiple design styles and functionalities were created on a case-by-case basis at different pricing tiers.

Familiar Feeling

I worked on developing HYLO for Android devices using Java, Android SDK, and Processing. iOS version soon to be released in tandem with Android.

Hylo was initially designed for large format displays but it has been extended to work on mobile or even be played in pairs.
